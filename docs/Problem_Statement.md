# Problem Statement

Urban waste management often relies on manual inspection, delayed reporting, and inconsistent disposal guidance. People may not know whether an item should be reused, recycled, or safely discarded, while municipalities lack a single place to review cleanup reports and prioritize responses.

Smart Waste Analyzer addresses this gap by combining object detection, sustainability guidance, and municipal reporting into one workflow. The system helps users identify waste, discover practical reuse and recycling paths, and report cleanup locations with geo-tagged evidence.
